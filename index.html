The result is the k92 headphones with an easily adjustable head band, weighing only 200g and with a modern retro style

HEAD SCAN & MODELING

Using several different 3d scans from different ethnicities and gender, average dimensions were extracted as a starting point for the modeling

The modeling was then made on a tilted sphere where the sphere / lens normal has an axial tilt of 8.3 degrees in the x-plane and 15 degrees in the y-plane - staying within industry standards for eyewear

Haglöfs Radius

HAGLÖFS Knife

This knife was created for Outdoor Equipment company Haglöfs during my time at the Umeå Institute of Design as part of a form language project.

After breaking down the companys core form values, a moodboard was created to communicate the direction of the design and a sketching phase resulted in a few key concepts from which a final proposal was made as a physical prototype, and then refined digitally as a CAD model.
